      Return & Replacement Policy

      We stand by the quality of our products and services. This policy outlines how replacements, refunds, and support requests are handled so you can purchase with confidence.

      1. Eligibility Window

      Return requests for refurbished and open-box hardware must be raised within seven (7) days of delivery. Brand-new items follow OEM return windows. Services and software licences are non-refundable once activated, unless specified in the agreement.

      2. Condition Requirements

      • Products must be returned with all accessories, packaging, and serial labels intact.
      • Data must be backed up and wiped before handover. Integrated IT Solution is not responsible for data loss.
      • Physical damage caused by mishandling voids the return eligibility.

      3. Approval Process

      Contact support@integrateditsolution.in with order details, issue description, and supporting photos/videos. Our engineering team validates the claim within two (2) business days.

      4. Resolutions

      • Replacement: Like-for-like replacement is prioritised when stock is available.
      • Repair: For components under warranty, repairs are coordinated with OEM or in-house labs.
      • Refund: Issued if replacement is not feasible. Refunds are processed within seven (7) working days post-approval.

      5. Logistics

      Reverse logistics are arranged by Integrated IT Solution for eligible claims. Clients are requested to pack goods securely to avoid transit damage.

      6. Service Engagements

      For AMC or project services, issues are resolved under documented SLAs. Credits or rework are provided when we fall short of agreed deliverables.

      7. Escalation

      If you need additional support, escalate to care@integrateditsolution.in. We respond within one (1) business day.

      Effective date: 11 November 2025.
